Why GW?

I decided to take the GW Bootcamp because I need a career change. I currently work as an Admin Assistant in fundraising and it's just not for me anymore. After having my son, I realized I was bored and needed to rethink my career track. Since I had taken several graphic design courses, I decided let's look at what really creates the design of the web page. When I started playing around with the basics of coding and realized it was something that utilized my love of problem solving and creating something. Of course I realized the best way to really pick up new skills was a bootcamp and GW looked like one of the best out there that had everything I needed as someone new to the field. So here I am ready to learn!
